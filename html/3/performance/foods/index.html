Top 10 food to eat in Sweden:
  • fika (not a food but do it anyways)
  • semla. King Adolf Frederick, ruling over Sweden during a time when he had basically no control, decided to just spend his time eating. One day, after a hearty dinner of lobster, sauerkraut, and kippers, washed down with champagne, he consumed 14 servings of semla. Shortly after, he died, probably of a stroke (the official diagnosis was "digestions problems," but this was the early 18th century).
