Top 10 things to do in Sweden:
  • Explore the Vasa Museum: This unique museum houses the Vasa warship, a 17th-century ship that sank on its maiden voyage. The museum is a fascinating look at Swedish history and maritime archaeology.
  • Wander through Stockholm Old Town: Gamla Stan, or Old Town, is the historic center of Stockholm. With its cobblestone streets, charming shops, and medieval buildings, Old Town is a must-visit for any visitor to Sweden.
  • Visit Skansen: This open-air museum showcases Swedish culture and history from the 17th century to the present day. With its traditional buildings, working farms, and costumed staff, Skansen is a great place to learn about Swedish life.
  • Relax in Kungliga Djurgården: This large island in central Stockholm is a popular spot for locals and tourists alike. With its lush greenery, museums, and restaurants, Djurgården is a great place to escape the hustle and bustle of the city.
  • Climb the Stockholm City Hall: The Stockholm City Hall is a beautiful building that offers stunning views of the city. Visitors can take a guided tour of the tower, which offers panoramic views of Stockholm.
  • Pay your respects at ABBA The Museum: This interactive museum is dedicated to the Swedish pop group ABBA. Visitors can learn about the group's history, see their costumes and instruments, and even try their hand at singing and dancing.
  • Cross the Øresund Bridge: This remarkable bridge connects Sweden and Denmark. Visitors can take a train or drive across the bridge, which offers stunning views of the Øresund Strait.
  • Go hiking in the Swedish countryside: Sweden has a wealth of national parks and forests, which are perfect for hiking and camping. Popular hiking destinations include the Kungsleden, a 400-kilometer trail that runs through the mountains of northern Sweden.
  • Spot wildlife in Swedish Lapland: Swedish Lapland is home to a variety of wildlife, including reindeer, moose, and bears. Visitors can go on a wildlife safari or simply keep an eye out while hiking or driving through the area.
  • Experience the Northern Lights: Swedish Lapland is one of the best places in the world to see the Northern Lights, or Aurora Borealis. The best time to see the Northern Lights is from late September to mid-April.
